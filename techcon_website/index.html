New venue!

TechCon 2024 will not be held at the Jacob K. Javits Convention Center this year; instead, it will take place at the New York Expo Center. The decision was made to accommodate our growing attendance and to provide ample space and world-class facilities that meet our high standards. This larger venue ensures a more comfortable and dynamic environment for networking, workshops, and presentations, enhancing the overall experience for all attendees.

Code of Conduct

TechCon 2024 is committed to providing a respectful and inclusive environment for all participants. We expect all attendees, speakers, sponsors, and volunteers to uphold high standards of professionalism and mutual respect. Harassment or discriminatory behavior of any kind will not be tolerated. Please engage with kindness and consideration, fostering a supportive atmosphere where diverse perspectives are valued and heard.
